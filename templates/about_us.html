About Us

Revolutionizing classroom attendance through secure, AI-powered biometric verification and precise geolocation.

Our Mission

We built {{ app_name }} to eliminate proxy attendance and streamline the administrative burden on teachers. By combining state-of-the-art face matching algorithms with strict geofencing, we ensure that attendance records are trustworthy, transparent, and easy to manage.

The Technology

Our platform utilizes a 128-dimensional facial descriptor vector system running purely on the edge device for privacy. It cross-references user identity with real-time GPS coordinates, ensuring that students can only mark their presence when they are genuinely inside the physical classroom environment.

Key Benefits

Zero Proxy

Biometric guarantees that the person marking attendance is the registered student.

Location Locked

Geofencing enforces physical presence inside the authorized attendance zone.

Lightning Fast

A class of 60 students can securely mark attendance simultaneously in seconds.
